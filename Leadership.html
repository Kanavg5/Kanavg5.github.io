Leadership Positions Held and Attempted


Robotics Competition Team

I was the only person that was able to code anything at the time of 4th grade. We were invited to join a robotics competition team, and I joined in. The others built the robot while I coded alone. In the sense that I was the only one able to code, and the fact that without code, a built robot will not function, I was a kind of leader in the team. I also lead the team in most of the rounds in the real competition.

Chess Club

I was made captian of Falcon Cove Middle School's Chess Club in 2020 for my prompt attendance and proactive role.

National Junior Honors Society

I ran for vice president for Falcon Cove's NJHS (National Junior Honors Society). In a close and intense race against me, one of the other candidates won the position. I attempted and I failed, but I still feel proud to be one of the few who decided to run for leadership.
