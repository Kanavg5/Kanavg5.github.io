Story/Poem of the Week!


Fall Leaves


Fall leaves dance in the wind
I take my view peacefully.
When I lie down to relax my mind
I do it easily.
I like the fall leaves
As they dance around in a circle.
The wind breathes down my neck
While the leaves run away and sparkle.
